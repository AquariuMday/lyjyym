Yanjun Li

I am currently a researcher at Fujitsu Research & Development Center, where I work on research and development in computer vision and intelligent systems.

I received my Ph.D. degree in 2025 from Hokkaido University, where I was a member of the Medical Image Analysis (MIA) Laboratory, supervised by Prof. Hiroyuki Sugimori. My doctoral research focused on medical image analysis and learning-based image understanding.

I received my M.S. degree in Mechatronics Engineering from The Australian National University in 2020.

I received my B.Eng. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Northeastern University in 2017.

My research interests include computer vision, medical image analysis, deep learning, and 3D perception, with a particular focus on practical and deployable systems that bridge learning-based methods and real-world applications.
